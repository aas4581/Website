About Me

Hi! My name is Arhan Salunke and I am currently a student at Northwestern University. I am pursing a double major in Computer Science and Economics with a goal of working in finance in the future. My personal interests include personal investing, trivia, traveling, intermural sports, automobiles, crosswords and consumer technology. I am from the Twin Cities and have worked here for the past few years, but I am hoping to work in a larger city in the future.

I come from a very small family, and both of my parents were immigrants, making me a first generation Asian American. Since my parents are immigrants we often traveled back and forth between the US and India a lot, which I suspect is what got me interested in travel, as well as aviation.
